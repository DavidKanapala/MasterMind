About

According to recent studies, nearly 1 in 6 people worldwide live with a disability. The MasterMind AI Assistant is designed to help individuals with hearing impairments, autism, and other disabilities better understand and express emotions. Using AI, it analyzes cues and provides instant feedback and support. We are also working in consultation with organizations such as Muhsen and The Arc to ensure our solutions remain accessible, inclusive, and aligned with the real needs of the disability community.
